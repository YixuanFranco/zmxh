Understanding Chakra Frequencies

The seven chakras each have an associated color, but did you know that they each have a unique frequency as well? Each chakra vibrates as a different frequency and learning to influence the chakra frequencies brings them into balance and clear energy blockages.

Everything about you is ‘alive’ with a vibrational frequency. Every thought, every emotion, every cell, every organ… everything that makes up ‘you’ is basically different vibrations clustered together. The chakras are the energy centers through which energy moves. When the chakras are open, everything is in balance and harmony.

Imagine the ‘system’ like a symphony orchestra. Every instrument in the orchestra has a different function and plays different notes; if they all played one not at the same time, then one note is all you would hear; but when they each play different notes in harmony with each other, together they make up an amazingly wonderful, pleasing symphony.

But if one instrument hits the wrong note, you energetically ‘hear’ an unpleasant dischord. Even though there’s nothing wrong with that note in and of itself, it is wrong for the symphony. That’s what happens when the chakras are blocked. Their frequency is disrupted, and they negatively affect the harmony of the ‘orchestra’. You are out of tune, and things start to go awry spiritually, mentally, emotionally and physically.

You know that you are drawn to certain sounds and repelled by others. It has been scientifically proven that unpleasant sounds increase your stress response! Of course what’s pleasant or unpleasant is personal preference; but your body responds to these sounds just as much as your mind and emotions!
